## üë• 2. User Roles & Permissions

### A. Shop Owners (Managers)
Managers have total control over their specific business identity.
- **Inventory Control:** Create, edit, and restock products.
- **Personnel Management:** Approve or revoke access for staff members.
- **Financial Auditing:** Access deep-dive reports, profit analysis, and PDF exports.
- **Subscription Management:** Upgrade the shop tier and handle billing.

### B. Personnel (Workers)
Workers are the "on-the-ground" operators.
- **Sales:** Use the "Sell Now" terminal to record customer transactions.
- **Inventory View:** Check stock levels and SKU details in real-time.
- **Support:** Send direct messages to the Shop Manager for assistance.

### C. Central Admin (Sydney)
The root administrator who governs the entire platform across all registered shops.
- **Payment Verification:** Manually verify Momo/Bank transfers to activate licenses.
- **Global Config:** Update platform-wide pricing and support contact details.
- **Security:** Manage master security protocols and audit logs.

## üîê 4. Access & Security

### Joining a Shop (Staff)
1.  Shop Manager provides the unique **SHOP CODE** (found in the Personnel tab).
2.  Worker registers using the "Staff Join" tab on the login screen.
3.  **Critical:** The Manager must then go to the "Personnel" tab and click the **Checkmark** to approve the worker.

### Recovering Access
If a password is forgotten:
1.  Click **"Forgot?"** on the login screen.
2.  Enter the registered email to receive a secure recovery link.
